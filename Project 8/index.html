Introduction

The Solar System is a vast system of celestial bodies held together by gravity, with the Sun at its center.

It includes planets, moons, asteroids, comets, and interplanetary dust.

The Sun

The Sun is a massive star that provides heat and light to all planets. It contains more than 99% of the Solar System’s mass.

Planets

The Solar System consists of eight planets classified into terrestrial, gas giants, and ice giants.

  • Mercury, Venus, Earth, Mars
  • Jupiter, Saturn
  • Uranus, Neptune

Moons

Moons are natural satellites orbiting planets. Earth’s Moon influences tides and stabilizes Earth’s rotation.

Space Exploration

Space exploration studies the universe using spacecraft, satellites, telescopes, and rovers.

Space Agencies

Space agencies play a key role in advancing space science and technology.

  • NASA – United States
  • ISRO – India
  • ESA – Europe
  • Roscosmos – Russia
  • CNSA – China

Future Missions

Future missions aim to establish human presence on Mars and explore habitable exoplanets.
